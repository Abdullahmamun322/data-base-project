Best-selling novelist Barbara Taylor Bradford dies.

has died at the age of 91. Published in 1979, A Woman of Substance sold 30 million

programme in Channel 4's history.It was the first of 40 novels by Taylor Bradford;
others include the Ravenscar, Cavendon and the House of Falconer series.
